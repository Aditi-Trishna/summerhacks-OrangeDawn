ABOUT THE SITE

This site is a repository for the Domestic Violence/Intimate Partner Violence resources,you'll find various articles,trivia and hotline numbers.

Our aim is to educate and raise awareness among the people regarding IPV/DV.

WHY IS IT IMOPORTANT TO TALK ABOUT IT?

  1. Almost one in three—have been subjected to intimate partner violence, non-partner sexual violence, or both at least once in their life (30 per cent of women aged 15 and older).
  2. As a result of Covid-related lockdowns,and the economic impact of the pandemic, reports of violence against women, particularly domestic violence, have increased in several countries.
  3. In the past 12 months, 243 million women and girls aged 15-49 have been victims of sexual and / or physical violence by an intimate partner relationship.

KNOW YOUR RIGHTS

  1. The Domestic Violence Act protects every woman in India irrespective of their marital status
  2. Any sexual conduct that abuses,humiliates or degrades a woman also amount to domestic violence.
  3. The law protects a woman in a live-in relationship from domestic violence.
  4. A woman can claim maintanence if husband has harrased her.
  5. A woman has two options against domestic violence:
    • Police complaint
    • Court Case
  6. Emotional abuse like repeatedly insulting or humiliating a woman also accounts for domestic violence.
  7. Domestic violence can be perpetrated by any person who is or was in a domestic relationship with the victim
